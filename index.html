I am Ronit April

Ronit April

License Massage Therapist and Body Worker


She first started practicing bodywork in 1997 in Israel and has returned to the table in 2014, practicing a larger array of bodywork modalities.
Ronit practices Therapeutic Swedish Massage and deep tissue,trigger-point as well as cranial sacral, footwork/reflexology and Reiki.

Ronit targets the places of pain or injuries and uses her hands and her box of tools to create a change.
The goal of her work is: "to listening to what is it the body's need, and take the time to get there, to create a shift together with the client."

Ronit believes in the body's ability to heal itself if given the support and assistance it needs. She acts as a facilitator in this process and support each person in his or her process.Ronit touch and experience is there to open the gate to that state of well-being.

Ronit is committed to her passion of massage and bodywork and continues to learn new methodologies and evolve as she navigates the path of her work.
