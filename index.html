About Ronit


When you’re in pain, it’s not just your body that’s in pain, it’s the whole of you.

I use massage to help bodies function more fully. It’s gratifying to see people leave their pain and tension behind on the table. Not only do they have more physical function, but with this renewed physical ease there’s more emotional flexibility. You can enjoy life fuller.

I was certified in Bodywork in 1998. Since then I’ve become certified in a number of powerful modalities, from Deep Tissue, to Reflexology, Cranio-sacral, and more. In each session, I use this anatomical knowledge and training along with patience, empathy and instinct.

For me, massage is a way of solving problems. Whether you come in with headaches, stiffness or chronic back pain, I listen closely with my hands to find what’s needed. If I recognize that something feels different, I’ll explore it, have you breathe into it. Together we’ll work to clear the tension, bring fresh blood, fresh nutrients, and, most importantly, bring the muscles back to a fuller function.

Bodywork is a powerful way to bring about change. It helps you feel more relaxed, more at ease, more grounded and balanced. This is a beautiful thing to bring into the rest of your life.

Why get a massage?

How do I know which type of massage to get?
If you don’t have a preference, don’t worry about deciding. Let me know what’s bothering you and I’ll incorporate several modalities into your massage.

I have a sports injury, can you help?
It depends on the injury. I’m good with runners, cyclists, and helping high performing athletes release tension. I can address issues like rotator cuff or shoulder injuries, anything, as long as it’s not a tear. If I can’t help you, I’ll refer you on.
